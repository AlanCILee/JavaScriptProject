About Us

In today’s competitive real estate market, the key to successfully buying and selling does not stop at just hiring a hard-working and experienced realtor. A solo agent can only handle as much work as one person can, no matter how capable he or she is. Having been successfully established in the industry since 1990, Home Property has seen many changes in the Metro Vancouver real estate market. In a fast paced market like the one we see today, efficiency is crucial in determining the success or failure of the sale of a property. With his determination to provide clients with the best service there is and to deliver the quickest and smoothest sales, Home Property has effectively built a team of real estate professionals who possess diverse, yet compatible skills, professional knowledge and ethnic backgrounds. Together with our team of professionals, Home Property has successfully helped thousands of home-owners in Metro Vancouver achieve their dreams.
